System Overview

Teacher-AssistantAI addresses key challenges in teacher support, providing real-time, personalized guidance. It integrates CRP, BRP, ARP, and teachers in a single digital coaching model. Teachers can ask questions via voice or text, and AI provides context-aware, subject-specific solutions.

How VEDA Helps the Education System
Teacher Support
• Acts as a digital teaching assistant, giving instant help for lesson planning and classroom doubts.

• Teachers select class, subject, and topic, so guidance is context-specific, not generic.

• Provides step-by-step explanations, activities, and teaching strategies in real time.

• Displays official notices and guidance on the same dashboard.

• Doubts can be asked via text or voice, saving preparation and classroom time.

• Works smoothly on mobile devices, making it practical inside classrooms.
CRP,BRP,ARP Support
• Provides one common platform for CRP, BRP, and ARP to coordinate academic support.

• Helps CRPs guide teachers, share notices, and track school-level activities.

• Enables BRPs to monitor CRP performance using dashboards and analytics.

• Supports ARPs in sharing subject expertise, resources, and best teaching practices.

• Reduces manual reporting and paperwork through digital tracking.

• Improves planning, monitoring, and decision-making using real-time data.
Challenges Solved

Limited CRPs

Reduces dependency on a small number of CRPs for teacher support.

Infrequent School Visits

Provides digital guidance to replace rare physical visits.

Delayed Problem Resolution

Enables timely interventions through AI-based support.

Lack of Real-Time Support

Teachers receive immediate advice for classroom challenges.

No Single Platform

Connects Teacher–CRP–BRP–ARP in one integrated system.
