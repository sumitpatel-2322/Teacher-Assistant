System Overview

Teacher-AssistantAI addresses key challenges in teacher support, providing real-time, personalized guidance. It integrates CRP, BRP, ARP, and teachers in a single digital coaching model. Teachers can ask questions via voice or text, and AI provides context-aware, subject-specific solutions.
